Basics

Name Li Li
Label Assistant Professor
Email lli18@uwyo.edu
Url https://lililab-uwyo.github.io/
Summary Our lab investigates how the brain senses and responds to peripheral metabolic signals to maintain energy balance. We are particularly interested in the gut–brain communication axis, focusing on how serotonin (5-HT) and its receptors (e.g., Htr1b) in hypothalamic neurons influence feeding behavior, energy expenditure, and body weight regulation.
